Chatterboxes

Chatterboxes

An augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) tool, giving a voice to those who may not have one. Search for symbols and pictures, create and edit your collection of symbols and build sentences that can be spoken out loud by speech synthesis. Chatterboxes can be used as a tool for speech modeling, sentence writing,or used to reinforce meaning for those who have English as an additional language. As Chatterboxes is a progressive Web App, it can be easily downloaded to a mobile device, free of charge, and uses open source symbols making this an accessible tool for those who need it.

Click Tap Beats

Make drum rhythms by clicking the steps in the sequencer. Add effects and pads to your beats by triggering sounds using your keyboard! Click Tap Beats utilises the Web Audio API and Tone JS to bring music creation to your browser.

Complimenter

Ever needed the your own cheerleader to help you feel confident about your outfits? Complimenter allows you to upload a photo of your look, and gives you a compliment in the style of Jonathan Van Ness from Queer Eye! Using image recognition, JVN can give you specific compliments depending on the garment or colour you're wearing.
